About Me

As a raised Oregonian I have the typical outdoor hobbies. I am a hiker, a snowboarder, a skateboarder and a surfer. Having gone snowboarding at leat once a month for the last 14 months, i'd say snowboarding is my favorite. Before this bootcamp started I was working at a ski resort in Eastern Washington as a member of the park crew building and grooming the park features. I came back to Oregon right before the New Year as I found out that I am pregnant. Due a month after we finish bootcamp!

I accomplished a Bachelors of Science in Microbiology and Chemistry where I went on to work in clinical trial phase pharmaceuticals to working in the cannabis industry as an extraction technician to working in quality control at a hazelnut production facility. I have a wide range of skills associated with my degree and have put that to in a few different industries. My goal with attending and completing this bootcamp if to be able to utilize my knowledge in a different way by being able to create and or help create ways of making scientific processes more streamline, accurate, or efficient.
